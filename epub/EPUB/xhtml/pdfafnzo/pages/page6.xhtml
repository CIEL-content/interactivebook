SCORM-based Learning Projects
CIEL’s SCORM based learning modules seek to boost and retain the concepts among the learner,
while enabling appropriate tracking and testing, aligned to your LMS and SCORM guidelines.
Talking Head Videos with Experts: Experts from the industry sharereal-life their
insight and expertise on various subject areas based on their experiences.
Simulations: A real environment (through simulations and stories) to teach com-
plex tasks or processes that are difﬁcult to communicate with just words on a screen.
Long Format E-courses: Interactive content, seat time of 30-60 min focused on a
speciﬁc topic with interspersed quizzes and knowledge checks.
Gamiﬁcation: Use the game format to administer quizzes and assessments in order
to foster learner engagement and retain learner enthusiasm.
